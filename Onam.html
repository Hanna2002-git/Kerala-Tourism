    Onam

    Onam is our national festival celebrated in the state of Kerala
    The 10-day-long festival Onam celebrates the homecoming of the Asura King Mahabali from the Patal Lok. Sadya is a popular traditional feast served during Onam.
    People in the southern state of Kerala are celebrating Onam to mark the harvest season and the end of the monsoon.

    The floral Rangoli, known as Onapookkalam, Athapookkalam or just Pookkalam,[40] is made out of the gathered blossoms with several varieties of flowers of differing tints pinched up into little pieces to design and decorate patterns on the floor, particularly at entrances and temple premises like a flower mat



    Sadhya is a traditional Kerala meal, essentially vegetarian, served on a banana leaf. A typical Sadhya can have two to three dozen dishes, which may sometimes go up to 64!



    There are some cutural programs are also conducted.Such as 'Thiruvathira','Pulikali'
